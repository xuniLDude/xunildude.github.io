Superstrate and Subtrate languages

Superstrate and Subtrate are lingustic terms used to describe how languages in bilingual communities affect each other.

Strate Details
Subtrate language It is a less dominant language that influences a socially dominant language. A substrate language may affect the grammars of new varieties such as pidgin and creole
Superstrate language A superstrate language is believed to have a major influence on the vocabulary of a new variety (pidgin and creole)
