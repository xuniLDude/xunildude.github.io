Words (lexemes) are built from morphemes - smaller parts with own meaning. Compounding and derivation are word building processes. Inflection grammatically adjusts words.
5 Words that go together (Idioms)
English relies on collocations (gloss/gloss_collocation.html) words that naturally go together. This relationship is often not abritrary. An idioms is a string of words in which the words collocate strongly, often to a degree such that the meaning of the idiom is unrelated to the meaning of individual words.
